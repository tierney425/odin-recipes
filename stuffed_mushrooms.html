Stuffed Mushrooms

Description

Stuffed mushrooms are my favorite appetizer. The recipe comes from my mom.

Ingediants

  • 1 Box of Mushrooms
  • 1 Tube of Jimmy Dean's Sausage
  • 1 Cup of Mayo
  • 1 Bag of Parmasean Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Prepare the mushrooms by removing their stems.
  3. In a large skillet, cook up the sausage and break into tiny pieces. Press any excess oil out of Sausage with a paper towl
  4. In a large bowl, Mix the Sausage with the Mayo and the Cheese
  5. Stuff the mushrooms with the mixture
  6. Cook in oven for 15 minutes or until done
