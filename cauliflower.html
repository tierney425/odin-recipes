Special Cauliflower

Description

This is a special cauliflower dish that will blow your mind!

Ingediants

  • 1 Head of Cauliflower
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Bag of Parmasean Cheese
  • 2 Tbsp Butter

Steps

  1. Boil the cauliflower until cooked, about 10 minutes
  2. Beat the eggs, mix in parmasean to create a slurry
  3. Cut the cauliflower into bite sized pieces
  4. Coat the cauliflower in the slurry
  5. Melt the butter in a skillet over medium low heat
  6. Fry the cauliflower, turning constantly until golden brown
